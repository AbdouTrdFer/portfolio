internship management

Creation of intern management web application. The intern management web application project aims to meet the specific needs of monitoring and management of internships within the Polytechnic School of Engineering. The accounts for supervisors and interns are added by the administrator, which allows management centralized and secure access. At the end of the internship, the intern can attach his report, and the supervisor can consult this report and evaluate the work using a rating system. The administrator also has the possibility of generating certificates for completed internships, integrating a unique QR code which, once scanned, directly displays the trainee's certificate concerned. Internship subjects are assigned by supervisors, while the administrator manages the assignment of trainees to supervisors.
